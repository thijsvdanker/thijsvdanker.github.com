_ KIDD

{% include product-details.md role=page.role client=page.client tech=page.technology site=page.website %}

KIDD is a platform to make and present lessons about art in the area. The lessons are targeted at middle school children. It focusses on creating cultural awareness by showing different kinds of art that is available in their own town.

As the developer it was my task to:
  • Develop an interactive map with art locations
  • Develop a lesson viewer for 'smart-boards'
