Mister Motley is an online magazine on Art and Everyday life. There are five different sections that cover different topics in their own style. There's room for great stories about art and everyday life, a diary with events, book reviews, q&a, and an academy for scholars. Good visuals is an important part of communcating art, and has received special attention throughout the site.

As the developer it was my task to:
  • Implement the design into a responsive theme
  • Develop content managent workflow
  • Develop slideshow and carousel integration
